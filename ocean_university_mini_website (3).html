Ocean University of Sri Lanka

National University established by Act of Parliament to develop competent human resources for Marine, Fisheries, Maritime and Blue Economy sectors of Sri Lanka.

About the University

Ocean University of Sri Lanka (OUSL) is a state university operating under the Ministry of Education. The University is mandated to provide nationally and internationally recognized education and training in fisheries, marine, maritime and allied disciplines.

Vision

To be a centre of excellence in ocean-based education, training, research and innovation.

Mission

To develop skilled and competent human resources through quality education, industry partnership and applied research for sustainable development of the blue economy.

Academic & Training Programmes

Diploma Programmes

Diploma in Logistics Management, Aquaculture, Seafood Management, Diving Technology, Maritime Operations and related fields.

Degree Programmes

Bachelor degree programmes in Fisheries & Ocean Sciences, Marine Engineering Technology and allied disciplines.

Short Courses & CPD

Competency-based short courses for industry professionals, youth, entrepreneurs and public officers.
